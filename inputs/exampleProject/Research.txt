This is a solid experimental setup. Studying Interferon-beta (IFN-$\beta$) stimulation in PBMCs is a classic model for understanding immune activation and transcriptomic shifts.

Below is a comprehensive single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq) analysis plan, moving from standard processing to advanced trajectory inference to capture the "evolution" of the immune response.

---

## 1. Project Overview
* **Sample Groups:** Control (Unstimulated) PBMCs vs. IFN-$\beta$ Stimulated PBMCs.
* **Objective:** Identify cell-type-specific responses to IFN-$\beta$ and reconstruct the transition states of immune cells from a resting to an activated state.

---

## 2. Phase I: Basic Bioinformatic Processing
This phase focuses on turning raw reads into a clean, annotated cell-by-gene matrix.

### Data Preprocessing & QC
* **Alignment:** Use `CellRanger` (for 10x Genomics) or `kb-python` to align reads to the reference genome (e.g., GRCh38).
* **Quality Control:** Filter cells based on:
    * **Library size** (Total UMI counts).
    * **Detected genes** (Avoid "empty" droplets or doublets).
    * **Mitochondrial percentage:** High % often indicates stressed/dying cells.
* **Normalization:** Use `LogNormalize` or `SCTransform` (Seurat) to account for sequencing depth variations.

### Integration & Dimensionality Reduction
* **Integration:** Use Harmony to integrate the Control and Stimulated datasets, removing batch effects while preserving biological variance.
* **Clustering:** Perform PCA followed by Shared Nearest Neighbor (SNN) clustering.
* **Visualization:** Use **UMAP** or **t-SNE** to visualize the cell populations (T-cells, B-cells, Monocytes, NK cells, etc.).



---

## 3. Phase II: Differential State Analysis
Once clusters are defined, we quantify the impact of IFN-$\beta$.

### Cell Type Annotation
* Use canonical markers andautomated tools like 'Typerlist'.

### Differential Expression (DE)
* **Consensus Markers:** Find markers for each cell type.
* **Stimulation Markers:** Perform "Condition vs. Control" DE analysis within each cluster to identify **Interferon-Stimulated Genes (ISGs)** like *ISG15*, *IFI6*, and *MX1*.
* **Pathway Enrichment:** Apply **GSEA** or **Over-Representation Analysis (ORA)** using the MSigDB Hallmark or Reactome databases to confirm viral response pathways.
